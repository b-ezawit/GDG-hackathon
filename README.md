# DeltaSense: The Reality Check Engine

DeltaSense is a high-stakes gap analysis platform designed to expose the "Delta" between your current state and your target goals. Whether you are a student comparing your notes to a course syllabus or a job seeker aligning your résumé with a competitive job description, DeltaSense uses a combination of lexical heuristics and AI-powered thematic mapping to visualize exactly what you are missing. With a 3D-accelerated dashboard featuring interactive radar charts and a visceral "Survival Meter," users get an immediate, data-driven reality check on their readiness for the next big challenge.

The platform's standout feature is the "Hot Seat"—an interactive interrogation module that turns static analysis into active practice. Once your weak points are identified, DeltaSense launches a relentless AI-powered interrogation session that grills you on your specific gaps under the pressure of a ticking timer and a rising Stress Level meter. Built with Next.js, Framer Motion, and OpenAI, DeltaSense doesn't just show you the map of your knowledge; it forces you to defend it, making it the ultimate training ground for high-pressure interviews and exams.

## Getting Started

1. **Clone the repo**
2. **Install dependencies:** `npm install`
3. **Set up Environment:** Copy `.env.example` to `.env` and add your `OPENAI_API_KEY`.
4. **Run the app:** `npm run dev`
5. **Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000)**
