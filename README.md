AmICooked: The Reality Check Engine

AmICooked is a high-stakes gap analysis platform built to reveal the brutal difference between where you are and where you need to be. Whether you're a student comparing your notes against a course syllabus or a job seeker matching your résumé to a competitive job description, AmICooked combines lexical heuristics with AI-powered thematic mapping to uncover exactly what’s missing. Through a sleek 3D-accelerated dashboard packed with interactive radar charts and an intense “Cooked Meter,” users get an instant, data-driven reality check on how prepared they truly are for their next challenge.

What makes AmICooked stand out is the “Hot Seat” — an AI-driven interrogation arena that transforms passive analysis into active pressure training. After identifying your weak points, the platform launches a relentless questioning session designed around your exact knowledge gaps. With a ticking timer, rising stress indicators, and adaptive follow-up questions, users are pushed to think fast and defend what they know under pressure. ## Getting Started

1. **Clone the repo**
2. **Install dependencies:** `npm install`
3. **Set up Environment:** Copy `.env.example` to `.env` and add your `OPENAI_API_KEY`.
4. **Run the app:** `npm run dev`
5. **Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000)**
